Agent

DQNAgent constructor now accepts an optional device parameter, allowing callers to override the default CUDA/CPU auto-detection. This enables explicit MPS (Apple GPU) and CPU device selection from the training script.

  • src/agents/dqn_agent.py: Added optional device parameter to __init__; if provided, uses it directly instead of auto-detecting CUDA/CPU.

Training

Major enrichment of the training pipeline: enhanced evaluation metrics (misses, rally_length, hit_ratio), progress reporting with speed/ETA, device selection with validation, training completion summary, and a new 224-line chart generation script.

  • src/train/train_dqn.py: Added resolve_device() for auto/cpu/mps/cuda selection with availability checks. Added _format_elapsed() and _format_eta() helpers. Train loop now tracks interval loss, reports progress every log_every_steps with speed/ETA. Logs enhanced metrics (misses, rally_length, hit_ratio). Prints training completion summary with device, time, and play instructions. CLI gains --device and --total-steps flags.
  • src/train/evaluate.py: Evaluation now collects misses and rally_length per episode. Computes aggregate hit_ratio (hits/(hits+misses)). Returns mean_misses, mean_rally_length, and hit_ratio in results dict.
  • src/train/plot_training.py: New 224-line script generating 4 matplotlib charts: hit_ratio vs steps (log scale), training speed vs steps, hit_ratio vs wall-clock time, and rally_length vs steps. Auto-discovers runs from artifacts/ directory. Supports multi-run overlay with device-labeled legends.

Observability

MetricsLogger now records wall_time and elapsed_s in every JSONL log line, enabling wall-clock analysis and chart generation.

  • src/obs/logging.py: Added time import and _start_time field. Every log_metrics call now includes wall_time (epoch seconds) and elapsed_s (seconds since logger creation) in the JSONL output.

Config

Added 10 new Makefile targets for full/1M training, play-trained, verify-learning-strict, and chart generation. Two new YAML training configs for 100k and 1M step runs.

  • Makefile: Added targets: train-full, train-full-cpu, train-full-mps, play-trained, verify-learning-strict, train-1m, train-1m-mps, train-1m-cpu, plot-training. Updated .PHONY list.
  • configs/dqn_minipong_1m.yaml: New config: 1M total steps, lr=0.0005, epsilon_decay_steps=500k, replay_capacity=100k, eval_every=100k, log_every=10k. Extended training for catastrophic forgetting experiments.

HIGH Catastrophic forgetting at 1M steps needs investigation

The 1M MPS training peaked at 600k steps (hit_ratio=1.0, rally_length=24.0) then collapsed by 1M steps. This is documented in the spec but not mitigated. Consider: learning rate scheduling, early stopping based on hit_ratio plateau, or replay buffer prioritization.

## configs/dqn_minipong_1m.yaml, total_steps: 1000000, lr: 0.0005, epsilon_decay_steps: 500000
Failure scenario
Future training runs past 600k steps waste compute and produce worse agents than shorter runs.
Resolution
Early stopping or LR scheduling prevents collapse, allowing agents to maintain peak performance through extended training.
